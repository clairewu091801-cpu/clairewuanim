PAINTED WORLD: NEO-VERSAILLES

Indie Game (2023) | Animation Lead

A FIRST-PERSON ADVENTURE PUZZLE RPG INFUSED WITH HORROR ELEMENTS, SET IN AN ALTERNATE VERSION OF VERSAILLES, FRANCEâ€”NEO-VERSAILLES.

Once celebrated as the artistic and technological center of the world, Neo-Versailles descends into chaos as Demons of Despair emerge from art, unleashing destruction. Players assume the role of an Art Keeper, a guardian capable of exorcising these demons back into their painting vessels, transforming the wicked into beauty.

In this high-tension environment, players wield a spray can to solve puzzles and evade enemies, culminating in a climactic exorcism at the game's conclusion.

Directed by Sammy Goraiaga

Worked on the project as an Animation Lead, spearheading a team of 4 animators to deliver an original indie game with high-quality standards. Also coordinated cross-departmental timelines, facilitating streamlined communication and ensuring all deliverables were met on schedule and optimized asset management by creating and implementing an intuitive file-naming system.
